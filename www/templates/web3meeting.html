Less than 25% of business partners rate IT’s strategy and planning efforts as effective, and many IT strategies either offer vague plans to meet even vaguer goals, or go to the other extreme and present long lists of tactical projects that have little connection to the company’s objectives.

In this webinar you will learn how:

  1. the most effective IT chiefs of staff ensure their IT strategy is informed by business objectives and bring creativity into IT strategic plans.

  2. You will also be able to discuss the challenges and opportunities of IT strategic planning with the many other chiefs of staff who are participating in the webinar.
