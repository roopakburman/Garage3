Workshop: Accelerating Commercial Contracting

Executive Retreat
22 Jul 2015 (Chicago, IL)

Legal departments spend too much time managing low-value contracts, crowding out higher-value work. Learn to:
  • Allocate resources to the riskiest, highest-value contracts;
  • Increase the efficiency of contracting processes to accelerate growth; and
  • Minimize risk by organizing contract records and tracking contract milestones.
