Business leaders look to a widening range of emergent and niche vendors to support capabilities that drive competitive advantage. CIOs are well aware of the opportunities that collaboration with partners outside of the traditional vendor portfolio can bring. Unfortunately, many IT organizations still lack an effective process for recognizing and acting on these opportunities.

In this webinar you will learn how to:

  1. Take a principled approach to diversifying the vendor portfolio

  2. Select vendors for their ability to drive business opportunity creation and innovation
