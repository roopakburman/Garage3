Adaptive IT: Leading for Speed and Flexibility

In order to keep up with broad-based and fast-changing technology advancements, the best IT organizations must constantly adapt to thrive in any environment, without stipulating a specific end-state.

IT's inability to deliver at the new speed-of-business creates a competitive disadvantage for the organization. In fact, our research shows that two-thirds of business leaders believe their organization responds too slowly to technology-enabled opportunities.
