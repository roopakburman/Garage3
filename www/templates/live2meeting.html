Join us for the 2015 Executive Meeting series, where CEB brings together true peers to discuss solutions to the most critical challenges facing CIOs today.

Sixty-three percent of business leaders believe their company responds too slowly to technology-enabled opportunities. In an era when the vast majority of organizational priorities rely on technology, and technology has become indispensable to develop, market, distribute and sell the company's products, IT's inability to speed up creates a competitive disadvantage.
